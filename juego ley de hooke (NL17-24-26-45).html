Í©ú WHAT IS HOOKE'S LAW? Í©ú

Hooke's Law, formulated by the English scientist Robert Hooke in 1660, describes the elastic behavior of bodies when a force is applied to them. This law states that "the deformation of a body is directly proportional to the applied force, as long as the elastic limit of the material is not exceeded."
